Specials

Besides being known for all the standard recipes for home-cooked food, at Hank's we are also well known for our special recipes, fresh baked goods and greek food. From homemade sticky buns to hot turkey sandwiches, from shiittake and cheese omelettes to spanakopita, no one beats Hank's Place in taste and variety.

Basic breakfast is served all day at Hank's. Eggs any style on the grill, breakfast meats, home fried potatoes, hot cakes, french toast & one omelette featured all day. 

Daily specials also available.
