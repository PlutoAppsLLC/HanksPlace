About Hank's Place

Hank's Place is located in Chadds Ford, Pennsylvania, the heart of Wyeth Country. Known for our homestyle cooking, we are one of the areas top resturants known for good food and friendly people! Hank's Place has been in business for over 16 years. Now owned an operated by Katie and Anthony Young, Hank's Place has been recognized over the years with numerous awards. Everything we serve is homemade and cooked fresh on site. Our breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus boast many favorite foods, freshed baked goods, as well as Hank's Place specialties.
